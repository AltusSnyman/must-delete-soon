FAQ

Roofing answers Perth homeowners ask most

Do you provide free roof inspections and quotes? +

Yes. Every project begins with a thorough inspection, photos, and an obligation-free quote so you can make an informed decision.

Can you work around existing solar panels or A/C units? +

Absolutely. Our team is trained to carefully remove, protect, and reinstall solar, antennas, and HVAC units without disrupting warranties.

What roofing materials do you use? +

We use Australian-made tiles, Colorbond steel, premium primers, and membrane coatings that are engineered for Perth’s climate.

Do you offer interest-free financing? +

Yes, through Brighte Finance. Most clients get approval within 24 hours, with flexible repayment schedules.

How long does a full restoration take? +

Most restorations are completed within 3–5 days depending on roof size, weather, and coating cure times.

Are you licensed and insured? +

Yes, Nxtgen Roofing is fully licensed, insured, and compliant with WA building regulations. Certificates of currency are available on request.
